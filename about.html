About

Who Are We?

Datacode is a technical programming and e-learning community dedicated to expand the coding knowledge among the coding enthusiasts. Our vision is that every person who is interested in coding should have the opportunity to learn. We organize contests and daily quizzes to encourage the learners. Datacode also organizes the weekly Shoot Py_ competitions which has students engaged from all over India. Datacode increases diversity by reaching learners of all backgrounds and inspire them to keep learning. "Truth can only be found in one place: the code." Hence, we believe in focusing on a enhancing the way people code. All our efforts are concentrated on creating a platform where everyone, from beginners to expert can come and learn coding in a fun and easy.
